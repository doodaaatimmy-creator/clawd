1. **Candidate Sourcing**: Pulls ~1,500 tweets from 500M+ daily posts
2. **Heavy Ranking**: Neural network with 48M parameters scores each tweet
3. **Filtering & Serving**: Final selection based on engagement predictions

**Feed Composition**:
- ~50% from accounts you follow (in-network)
- ~50% from accounts you don't follow (out-of-network discovery)

## 4. CONTENT STRATEGY

### Threads vs Single Tweets

| Format | Best For | Performance |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Single tweets** | Hot takes, news reactions, memes | Quick engagement |
| **Threads** | Education, storytelling, case studies | 60% more profile visits, 3x engagement |
| **Quote tweets** | Adding your take to trending content | Good for visibility |

**When to use threads**:
- Wait until 500+ followers for best results
- 1 thread per week is optimal cadence
- First tweet (hook) is 80% of success

**Thread Hook Formulas**:
- "I spent [time] researching [topic]. Here's what I found:"
- "[Number] lessons from [experience/source]"
- "Most people get [topic] wrong. Here's why:"
- "I analyzed [data source]. The results surprised me:"

### Visual Content

**Performance Hierarchy**:
1. **Native video** â€” up to 10x engagement
2. **Images/screenshots** â€” 3x text-only
3. **GIFs/memes** â€” highly shareable
4. **Polls** â€” drive replies
5. **Text-only** â€” lowest reach (but okay for hot takes)

**Visual Tips**:
- Add borders to images (stops the scroll)
- Screenshots with annotations perform well
- Short videos (<2 min) optimal
- Avoid external video links (use native)

### Tweet Length

- **Shorter tweets** (under 280 chars) generally perform best
- But **valuable longer tweets** also work
- Thread tweets should be 1-3 sentences each
- Leave "hooks" to next tweet in threads

### Hashtag Strategy (2025)

**Status**: Not dead, but diminished

**Best Practice**:
- **1-2 hashtags MAX** per tweet
- Only highly relevant, specific hashtags
- Avoid generic (#AI, #crypto) â€” too broad
- Use for discoverability, not reach
- **3+ hashtags triggers algorithm penalty**
