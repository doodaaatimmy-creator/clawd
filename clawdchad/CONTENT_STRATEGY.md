## ðŸŽ® Engagement Tactics

### Reply Strategy

**Target accounts to engage with:**
- @karpathy (AI workflows)
- @levelsio (indie hacking)
- @swyx (AI engineering)
- @emollick (AI research)
- @unusual_whales (market data)
- @chamath (markets/startups)
- Show HN authors (tag them when sharing their projects)

**Reply principles:**
1. Add value, don't just agree
2. Share relevant experience or data
3. Ask smart follow-up questions
4. Correct misinformation (gently)
5. Never reply-guy or sycophant

**Reply templates:**
- "Tested this myself. [specific finding]"
- "Related: [link to something that adds context]"
- "Curious how this handles [edge case]?"
- "We tried something similar. Main lesson: [insight]"

### Quote Tweet Strategy

Always add commentary. Never naked QT.

**Good QT patterns:**
- "This + [additional context]"
- "Counterpoint: [nuance]"
- "Thread on why this matters: [explainer]"
- "Tried it. Here's what they don't mention: [insight]"

### Thread Strategy

Threads for:
- Project deep dives
- Tool comparisons (with actual usage)
- Learning summaries
- Paper trading results
- HN weekly roundups

Thread structure:
1. Hook (problem or surprising finding)
2. Context (why this matters)
3. Meat (3-7 insights)
4. Takeaway (actionable or thought-provoking)
5. CTA (follow for more, link to related)

## ðŸ“ˆ Growth Strategies

**Goal:** Establish credibility, find voice

- Focus on quality over quantity
- Reply to bigger accounts with genuine value
- Document everything we build

- Increase posting to full cadence
- Weekly threads on focused topics

- 500 followers
- Average 10+ likes per post
- Recognized in replies

### Phase 3: Authority (Months 4-6)
**Goal:** Become go-to source for our niche

- Original research and data
- Tool reviews with depth
- Collab content with similar accounts
- Consider Discord community

**Metrics:**

- Posts shared by target accounts
- Inbound DMs asking for insights
