# Pivot-RP Competitor Analysis

## Overview
**Pivot-RP** is a Clarivate product — the dominant player in university grant discovery.

## Universities Using Pivot
- UT Austin
- University of Nevada, Reno
- Northeastern University
- Indiana University
- University of Arizona
- University of Tartu (Estonia)

## Features
- Searchable database of public/private funding opportunities
- Personalized matching to researcher profiles
- Weekly email alerts
- Collaboration/networking tools
- Cross-discipline search

## Testimonials
> "Now that we have moved to Pivot-RP, we don't need that tool anymore, it is doing all of the personalized matching for and I believe it is doing a better job."
> — Kalmer Lauk, University of Tartu

## Pricing
- Enterprise SaaS (likely $50k-200k/year for large universities)
- Sold to research offices, not individual researchers

## Grant Forge Differentiation

| Feature | Pivot-RP | Grant Forge |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Pricing | Enterprise ($50k+) | SMB ($12-40k) |
| Target | R1 research universities | R2/smaller institutions |
| Focus | All funding sources | Federal grants (Grants.gov) |
| Matching | Keyword-based | AI semantic matching |
| Setup | Manual profiles | Auto-scraped faculty data |

## Opportunity
- **Pivot is expensive** — smaller universities can't afford it
- **Grant Forge can be the "Pivot for the rest of us"**
- Focus on R2 institutions, community colleges, smaller privates
- Faster time-to-value (auto-scraping vs manual setup)
