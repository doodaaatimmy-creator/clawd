### What's Rising
1. **Vibecoding acceptance** - From joke to legitimate methodology
2. **MCP adoption** - Protocol winning the AI integration wars
3. **Browser automation** - Major focus (One Browser, Playwright MCP, LemonSlice)
4. **European sovereignty** - Growing concern driving new tools
5. **Benchmark culture** - TetrisBench, SnapBench, specific capability testing

### What's Plateauing
1. **Generic "AI wrapper" products** - Market saturated
2. **Chat-only interfaces** - Voice/video agents taking over
3. **Unstructured prompting** - Moving to protocol-based interaction

### What's Declining (Skepticism Growing)
1. **AI infrastructure claims** - Cloudflare Matrix debacle shows scrutiny increasing
2. **Unfettered AI crawling** - More sites blocking Claude, AI bots
3. **Model-as-moat thinking** - Protocol/tooling differentiation winning
